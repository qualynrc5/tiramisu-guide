More About Tiramisu

Welcome to page 2! Here we explore more details about tiramisu, including its history and variations.

Tiramisu originated in Italy in the 1960s and has become a world-famous dessert. There are many variations, including chocolate, strawberry, and matcha flavors.

Fun Facts About Tiramisu

  • The name "Tiramisu" means "pick me up" in Italian.
  • It was originally made without alcohol in some regions.
  • The dessert is traditionally served chilled.
